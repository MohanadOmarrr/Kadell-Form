How happy are you in your body?
Does it affect your mental health?


The effect of poor body image on mental goes beyond occasional negative thoughts. A study at Bradley Hospital found that people with weight preoccupations or body dysmorphic disorder display higher levels of symptoms for depression and anxiety, and are more likely to have suicidal thoughts.

Because of this, part of maintaining mental health involves also maintaining a healthy body image. By treating your body with respect and practicing self-love for your body, you can improve your mental health.
